My Cat Tiggy

Tiggy, a mischievous and energetic feline, was a beloved member of the family. His playful nature was infectious, often leading him on adventurous escapades outdoors, where he'd engage in playful battles with neighborhood cats. Indoors, Tiggy was equally as playful, using his paws to playfully stomp on feet as a way to demand attention or treats. A creature of habit, he enjoyed the comfort of his nightly ritual, curling up at the foot of the bed. Though his life was tragically cut short by a car accident, Tiggy's memory lives on, a testament to his spirited and playful personality.
